Rimworld

Overview

Rimworld is a space colony sim game by Tynan Sylvester and published by by Ludeon Studios. Your goal is to set up a colony and get off the planet. As you do your best to survive until you can leave you must stop sickness,raiders,weather and your own cololnists from destroying your colony

Gameplay

As a colony sim your main gameplay is gathering resources and building structures and defences to try and keep your colonists alive. you need to pick your starting scenario that determines your starting recources, pick what colonists you want to start with and your AI storyteller that will deterimnes what events occur to your colony and at what pace. after all that you pick your strating location and get to work. Your colonist have radom stats and traits that can help or hinder you in your attempts to build your colony and get off plantet. You have no direct control over your colonists but instead must set things up so they will complete their tasks. Various threat will forever plauge your colony everything from rabid animals, to raiders, to robots. Your colonists all have their own traits ranging from being a good jogger to being a cannibal all of who you have to try and get to work together to build a way off plantet.

Story

Rimworld does't really have a overall story or villain(s) the story as it were are what you deal with in the process of creating a colony.Your victories, your defeats, your struggles and your colonists are what create the story one that is just for you.

Summary

Rimworld is a very addictive and very fun game that i have spent many many hours on and will certianly spend many more with each run learing more and creating ever larger and more sophistacted colonies and should i ever run out of stuff to learn the game has great mod support!
